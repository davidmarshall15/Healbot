Reset HealBot


To set absolute defaults for Healbot on all Accounts and Realms, do the following in order:

NOTE: On step 5 for Classic replace _retail_ with _classic_ and for Classic Era replace _retail_ with _classic_era_

1: Export any skins/spells/buffs/debuffs you want to keep
2: Completely Logout of WoW
3: Download Reset_HealBot.zip from the HealBot Website
4: Unzip Reset_HealBot.zip to a temporary folder
5: Copy Reset_HealBot.bat into the World of Warcraft _retail_ folder (ie. C:\Program Files\World of Warcraft\_retail_)
6: Run Reset_HealBot.bat
7: Type “D” to delete Healbot’s WTF files
8: Logon to WoW
9: Import the settings exported at step 1 or setup Spells, Buffs, Cures and Skins
